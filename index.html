    How this works, there

    Four quick things before you start:

    1
    You'll see two teams at a time. Tap whichever one you think will finish higher in the 26/27 table. That's it — no need to think about the other 18 teams at once.
    2
    You'll get around 90–120 match-ups, not all 190 possible pairs — the engine works out the full table from a smart, minimal set of picks. The progress bar at the top shows roughly how many are left.
    3
    Genuinely unsure? Tap "Too close to call — decide for me" and it'll settle the pick using last season's finish, so you're never stuck. You can also tap "Undo last pick" if you tap the wrong team by mistake.
    ✓
    At the end, you'll see your full predicted table. Not quite right? Use the ▲▼ arrows next to any team to nudge it up or down — take your time, there's no rush. When you're happy with it, tap "Lock in my table" — this copies your table and takes you straight to the submission form, where you just paste it in to submit your entry.
